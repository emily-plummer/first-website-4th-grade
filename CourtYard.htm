The Courtyard

The courtyard is a very beautiful place. There are a lot of the important statues in it.

There was also a lot of very pretty plants there.

 

This is the courtyard. If you look hard you can see the fountain.

Here is the fountain.  It is very beautiful. There are trees all around it. They also have lights on the top. One other thing is that on the edges they have pots with flowers. This probably is a favorite thing about the mission.

 

 

Here is one of the many statues.

Here is where Father Jayme was buried.  He is the man who took over when Father Serra went back to New Spain.  He was killed when the mission was attacked by Indians in 1775.
