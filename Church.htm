The Church

 The church at San Diego de Alcala is very beautiful. They are still having Sunday church there. The church is one of the most important things in this mission. It is my favorite thing in the mission.

 

The church entrance is where the guests enter to be seated.

 

The church altar is where the people speak. The altar also has candles. When the church people leave they light the candles for people they want to remember. The church altar is also very beautiful.

The balcony is where the choir sings.

 

 

The Sacristy is where the priest kept his candles, clothes, and other supplies.
