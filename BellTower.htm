The Campanario           

The campanario is another important part of the mission. They rang the bells to wake people up, to announce when church was, and to announce when to go in for lunch, dinner, and breakfast.

                                           

                     

This is the front of the campanario. There are 5 bells and the biggest is the 1,200 pound Mater Dolorosa.

 

 

                                              

This is the back of the campanario.  When they want to ring the bells they just walk up the stairs.
